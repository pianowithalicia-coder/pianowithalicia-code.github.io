Studio Policy

Payment

Bank transfer and cash are both accepted. Payment is due at least 24 hours before each lesson to secure the booking. Lessons may be cancelled or the time slot offered to another student if payment has not been received in advance. Repeated late payments may result in the discontinuation of regular lessons.

Cancellation Policy

I understand that unexpected things can happen. If you need to cancel or reschedule a lesson, please let me know at least 24 hours in advance whenever possible. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice are charged in full, as the time has been reserved for you.

Missed Lessons

If a student does not attend a scheduled lesson without prior notice, the lesson will be charged in full.

Lesson Times

Regular weekly lesson times are reserved exclusively for each student. Lessons begin and end at the scheduled time. If a student arrives late, the lesson will still finish at the originally scheduled time.

Teacher Cancellation

If the teacher ever needs to cancel a lesson, it will be rescheduled or refunded.

Online Lessons

Online lessons are available if you are unable to attend in person.

Agreement

By booking lessons, you agree to the studio policies outlined above.
