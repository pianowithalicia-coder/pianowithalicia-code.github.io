About

I am a classically trained pianist with over a decade of formal musical education. My teaching combines technical discipline with musical imagination, helping students build both confidence and expressive ability.

I work with beginners, intermediate students, and those preparing for ABRSM examinations. Lessons are structured yet personalised, with careful attention to technique, musical understanding, and long-term development.
