About

I am a classically trained pianist with over a decade of formal musical education. My teaching combines technical discipline with musical imagination, helping students build both confidence and expressive ability.

I work with beginners, intermediate students, and those preparing for ABRSM examinations. Lessons are structured yet personalised, with careful attention to technique, musical understanding, and long-term development.

Studio 88

Why 88? A standard piano has 88 keys, and becoming familiar with the keyboard is one of the most important foundations of learning piano.

In my studio, students gradually learn to recognise notes both on the keyboard and on the music sheet, building confidence in reading, technique, and musical understanding.

Lessons are structured yet relaxed, supporting steady progress while helping students enjoy the process of making music.
